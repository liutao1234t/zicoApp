Privacy Policy

How zico handles photos, necklace references, prompts, generated looks, app activity, and support messages.

Effective July 1, 2026Support: support@zzico.click
Private by design. zico uses proprietary AI models designed, trained, deployed, and maintained by our own team for necklace styling and visual try-on. We do not use third-party AI models for the core styling experience, and we do not sell, rent, trade, or share user information for advertising or profiling.

01

Our Privacy Position

zico is designed for private necklace styling. We do not collect unnecessary personal information, and we do not sell, rent, trade, or share user information for advertising, data brokerage, cross-app tracking, or unrelated profiling. Our proprietary AI models are operated by us and are not third-party AI services.

02

Information You Choose to Provide

You may choose to provide a portrait, outfit photo, necklace reference, selected style, prompt, saved look, profile preference, support message, or purchase status. You are responsible for having permission to use any image you upload.

03

AI Processing

When you request an AI try-on or styling result, zico processes the image and styling choices you provide to generate the requested output. This processing is limited to delivering the feature, showing progress, maintaining safety, troubleshooting technical problems, and protecting service reliability.

We do not use uploads for facial recognition, biometric identification, identity verification, advertising targeting, or unrelated user profiling. Uploaded content is not treated as public model-training data.

04

Information Created by Use

To run the app, zico may process limited operational data such as chosen necklace categories, generation status, credit changes, app language, broad region, timestamps, device type, crash information, and support diagnostics. This information is used to operate and improve reliability, not to sell or expose your private styling activity.

05

How We Use Information

  • Provide necklace feeds, AI try-on, style guidance, saved looks, and support.
  • Maintain credit balances and purchase restoration where available.
  • Detect technical errors, fraud, unsafe requests, or abuse.
  • Respond to your questions at our support email.
  • Measure aggregated product performance without revealing individual private content.

06

Sharing

We do not sell or commercially share your information. We may disclose limited information only when necessary to operate infrastructure under confidentiality obligations, comply with law, protect rights and safety, process App Store purchases, or complete a business transfer where user privacy commitments continue to apply.

07

Retention and Deletion

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed to provide zico, maintain your requested history, complete support requests, comply with law, resolve disputes, and protect the service. You may delete available creations in the app or contact us to request privacy assistance.

08

Your Choices

You may avoid uploading portraits, decline optional permissions, remove saved looks where available, and contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy help. Some requests may be limited by security, legal, or technical requirements.

09

Children and Safety

zico is not intended for children below the minimum digital consent age in their region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children who are not allowed to use the service.

10

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse. No online service can guarantee absolute security, so please be thoughtful when uploading sensitive images.

Questions about zico or these terms may be sent to support@zzico.click.
